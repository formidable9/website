About Skyboi Formidable

Skyboi Formidable is a multi-talented artist, producer, and performer from Abuja, Nigeria. Active since 2010, he’s known for blending hip-hop, dancehall, and Afrobeat into a sound that’s fast, powerful, and unpredictable.

Beyond his music, Skyboi has mastered video production and drone cinematography, creating a fully independent creative ecosystem. His work showcases the art of multitasking — writing, performing, producing, and engineering his own tracks at a master level.

Popular releases include “Leader,” “God of War,” and “Let Me Through” — songs that highlight his lyrical precision and intensity. His performances are marked by rapid-fire verses, energetic rhythm, and a fearless message of determination.

Since the beginning, his goal has been simple: to inspire with truth, skill, and creative freedom. Skyboi continues to push boundaries as one of the fastest rappers in his region — proving that limits are only illusions.
